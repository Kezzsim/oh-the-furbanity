What is a city?

Let's go over some vocabulary before we really get started. A city is a place where a lot of people live. It's a metropolotan area.

What makes a good city?

A good city is one that has good infrastructure for people to get around on. Bike lanes, busses, subways etc.

What is the infamous 15 minute city?

A 15 minute city is a concept of having a city divided up into blocks that contain every service a person could need within a 15 minute walk of the housing in that block. Conservative Americans hate this idea because it means they can't drive their cars everywhere.

What is walkability?

walkability is the concept that most trips that people need to do to facilitate daily life should be within walking or biking distance.

Remember the Gucci of fursuits?

Zweitch was a group that proclaimed that they would make Fursuits with mass market appeal and labeled them high class luxury goods, similar to what developers do to real estate. The fandom was able to, as a whole, reject them and their attempts to commercialize and denature being furry. The goal of this conversation is to encourage people to remain unique and weird in a way that is not marketable.
